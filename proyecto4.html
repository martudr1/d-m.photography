Here are some tips to take into account while taking and editing them! September 30th, 2019

Hey! In this post we´ll teach you how to edit your photos with a Vsco style. First of all, you have to download this app and suscribe to it. After choosing the photo you want to edit, select this elements:

  • Fade: +2
  • Tint: +4

SUMMER EDITION

Golden Hour effect for your summer holidays pictures.October 27th, 2019

Summmer is almost here!And we want to teach you how to apply the Golden Hour effect on your photos easily and turn your pictures into better ones while being trendy.Follow this instructions!!:

  • Exposure: -1.2
  • Contrast: +0.3
  • Shadows: +1.8
  • Highlights: +11.6
  • Temperature: +1.1

Try adding Fade +1.1 to have a better summer look!!
